going on with Netty and their classpath. In particular, the `StackTraceSimplifier` class that is referred to was removed between 3.9.0 and 3.10.0 of Netty. Looking at the source of `InternalLoggerFactory` from say 3.9.0.Final, `StackTraceSimplifier` is used in the class initializer for `InternalLoggerFactory` (and this is not the case in 3.10.5.Final). If that class is not available, the class initializer for `InternalLoggerFactory` fails and that leads to a [`NoClassDefFoundError`](http://docs.oracle.com/javase/specs/jls/se7/html/jls-12.html#jls-12.4.2). The version of Netty that Elasticsearch depends on (3.10.5.Final) should not be looking for `StackTraceSimplifier` because it has been completely removed from Netty in that version.\n\nI took a cursory peek at YAJSW, and it appears to have a Netty dependency (maybe Netty 3.6.6.Final?).
